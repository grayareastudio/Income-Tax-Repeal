Contact

We read every message. A few notes before you write.

What we respond to

Corrections. If you've found an error in a statute reference, case citation, or historical characterization, please include the primary source. The Repository will note material corrections alongside the updated document.

Scholarly collaboration. Peer review, rebuttals in good faith, and new scholarship are welcome. Tell us what you're working on and how it connects.

Legislative briefings. We are available to brief Congressional and state-legislature staff on the constitutional record and the replacement mechanism.

Press. Include your outlet, your deadline, and the specific question you need addressed.

Organizing. Chapter and district organizers may request presentation materials and regional coordination.
